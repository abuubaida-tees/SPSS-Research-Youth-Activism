# Youth Political Activism Online: A Quantitative Study
**Author:** Abu Ubaida | **University:** University of Sargodha
**Tools:** SPSS 27, Excel, Chi-Square Testing, Regression Analysis

## Abstract
This study investigates the correlation between social media usage and political activism among 200+ university students. Using Pearson Correlation and Regression Analysis in SPSS, the study found a significant positive relationship (R = .207, p = .002) between sharing political content and participating in online campaigns, despite a lack of direct correlation with offline movement participation.
